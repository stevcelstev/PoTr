When

by Kay13

When eyes see beauty

And ears hear laughter

When the senses are awakened to the day's fresh

fragrances

When mouths speak only kind and guilding words

When arms embrace

When hands hekp

When fingers no longer point

When legs march only for peace

When feet dance in joy

When minds unite to create good

And there is respect for all

Then the heart and soul will sing with love.

MAGIC OF ALIVENESS

by Shilow38

magic abounds

allow it and feel so surrounded

feel the internal vibration of all that is

and all that ever was

filling the body from the bottom of the spine

an explosion

expansion of divine desiress

rippling up

and throughout every cell

every atom

up to the top of the head

shooting up to the sky

as a beautiful waterfall

that falls back upon thee

this is where you create

this is where you are born

this is where you thrive

this is why we are alive
