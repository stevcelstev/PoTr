Mirror, mirror

by Vidi8

mirror, mirror on the wall

was I always meant to fall?

roses white and dying light

silver's sweet forgiving bite

she'll ask "why"

no answers found

and I'll rot deep

beneath the ground

Every man

by Henry3

Every man has his secret sorrows

which the world knows not;

And often times we call a man cold

when he is only sad.
